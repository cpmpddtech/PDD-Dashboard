Finance Setup
One-time initialisation of Google Sheets structure

Step 1 — Initialise finance sheets

Creates Donors, Budgets, Budget_Donors, Line_Items and Expenditures sheets in your Finance Google Sheet. Safe to run multiple times.

What gets created
Donors — one row per donor (ABM, TearFund, USPG…). Set once, reused forever.
Budgets — one row per programme+diocese+period.
Budget_Donors — links donors to budgets with their MMK allocation.
Line_Items — the detailed budget rows with donor splits.
Expenditures — actual quarterly spending records with approval status.
Step 2 — Add your first donor
After initialising, go to Donors in the sidebar and add each of your partners:
ABM, TearFund, USPG, ARDF, ERD, Diocese/CPM, and any others.
Step 3 — Create your first budget
Go to Budgets → New Budget. Select the programme, diocese, and fiscal year. Then attach your donors and enter line items.
